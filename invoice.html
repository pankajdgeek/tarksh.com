The Problem

Financial chaos in small business

Small businesses in India juggle revenue from multiple sources, track expenses on spreadsheets, and struggle with GST compliance every quarter. The result:

  • Revenue scattered across multiple channels with no unified view
  • GST invoices created manually, often with missing or incorrect GSTIN data
  • Expenses tracked in WhatsApp messages, paper receipts, and spreadsheets
  • No clear view of profitability by client, project, or revenue stream
  • Vendor payments and reconciliation done by memory

What We're Building

One platform for your finances

Every invoice, every expense, every rupee — tracked, automated, and GST-compliant from day one.
