The Vision

Impact over income

Your phone should be a tool, not a trap. Beyond exists to help you reclaim your time and build healthier digital habits — without tracking your data, showing you ads, or charging a subscription.

Features

Everything you need to disconnect

Beyond respects your autonomy. Gentle nudges instead of harsh blocks. Awareness instead of restriction.

⏱

Focus Timer

Start timed focus sessions that block distracting apps. Stay in the zone without temptation.

🔓

App Blocker

Choose which apps to restrict. Beyond enforces your choices so you don't have to rely on willpower.

💭

Think Before Use

A gentle nudge before opening blocked apps. Makes you conscious of every choice without forcing you.

🌙

Bedtime Mode

Schedule evening wind-down with app blocking, screen dimming, and ambient sounds to help you sleep.

📈

Wellness Score

A weekly score based on your screen time, focus sessions, and habits. Track your progress over time.

📊

Daily Reports

See exactly where your time goes. Most-used apps, time saved, and patterns visualized clearly.
